Use this tool to get a latitude and longitude for your site.
You can:

  • Drag the red marker to a new location
  • Click anywhere on the map to move the marker to that location
  • Double-click anywhere on the map to zoom in
  • Click the 'Geocode this Address' button to get a lat/long from the address information
  • Enter a new latitude and longitude and click 'Refresh Map'
When done, click 'Save Latitude/Longitude' to continue.
