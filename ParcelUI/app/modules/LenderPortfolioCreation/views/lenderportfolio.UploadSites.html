Upload Site Spreadsheet

To upload an Excel spreadsheet containing Property Information, please complete the following steps:

  1. Save the Excel template : XLS or XLSX to your computer and open it from there.
  2. Fill out the spreadsheet with your site information, and save your changes.
    Each site must have data entered for address, city, and state/province.
  3. Click the "Browse" button below these instructions and select the file that you just saved.
  4. Click the "Upload File" button.
