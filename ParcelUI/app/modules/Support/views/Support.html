How do I Setup a New User?

PARCEL Support FAQs

Q:How do I Setup a New User?

A: Any user within a company can issue usernames and passwords from Account Management (link on the Main Menu). However, a particular user is unable to create a user at a higher access level than their own. For example, someone with Field Staff level access can only create another Field Staff user, whereas a Project Manager can create a Field Staff or Project Manager.

At the top of the page click on Change Password.

If no one is available to issue a username and password, please send a request to support@parcelplatform.com with the First and Last Name, email address, and whether the person is a Site Assessor or Project Manager.

Account Types and Capabilities:

Project Manager - Edit Default Language, Senior Review reports, and change the status of reports to 'Final'.

Senior Field Staff - Edit Default Language and enter data, but do not Senior Review or change status to 'Final'.

Field Staff - Enter data, upload documents, and change status.

Viewer - Simply need to view data, documentation and reports

The user will receive an email with login instructions. If the email does not arrive have them check their spam filter then call 866-475-1272 or email support@parcelplatform.com.

How do I Upload my Digital Signature?

PARCEL Support FAQs

Q:How do I Upload my Digital Signature?

A: Either click on your name upon logging into PARCEL or navigate to your profile within Account Management. Scan your signature into JPG format and use the upload tool to put that signature into your profile.

Most likely, you will need to edit the image. Aspect ratio should be 2:1 and the file should not be more than 50K. The image should be cropped to the boundaries of the signature and be no more than 3� wide.

How do I Upload Photos?

PARCEL Support FAQs

Q:How do I Upload Photos?

A: Go to the Photographs appendix within a report.

Individual photos may be uploaded into PARCEL by clicking the Browse button, selecting the photo and clicking the Upload File button. You may also upload all photos at once by putting them into a ZIP file and uploading the ZIP file.

If individual photos are uploaded, they may be captioned as they are chosen for Upload. If a ZIP file is uploaded, they may be captioned as part of the Upload.

Once the photos are in the appendix, their order may be modified by dragging and dropping to rearrange them.

A single blue arrow moves the photo one space and a double blue arrow moves it to the first or last photo in the group.

Clicking any photo will open it to full size on the screen. The captions can be edited and photos can be included, excluded or deleted by clicking the appropriate button.

How do I Create a Report?

PARCEL Support FAQs

Q:How do I Create a Deliverable Report?

A: To generate a complete report on PARCEL, click on Assemble Deliverable in the Tools Menu from within a report. At the bottom of the page click on Check All then Download PDF.

Other options include:

Emailing the report as an attachment - Direct PDF delivery. Based on file size, this may not be delivered due to email size reception settings.

Emailing a link with a secure password - Sends Email link with unique token, allows recipient to download directly from PARCEL to avoid email file size issues.

Hardcopy delivery - Will allow you to rely upon a third-party to print, bind and ship your reports anywhere in the country for next day delivery. (Please note that charges will apply for this service).

Note: If you just want the report minus the appendices, please use the Quick View Reports link from within the report.

Why is a Deliverable Missing Files?

PARCEL Support FAQs

Q:Why is a Deliverable Missing Files?

A: There can be multiple reasons why a deliverable is missing files.

The file is not in PDF format Files in other formats cannot be included in the PDF deliverable. To rectify, delete file and upload as PDF or check the Convert to PDF upon upload box.

The file is corrupt or unreadable Two common issues exist, if a document contains form fields, or if the document contains a digital signature, where you have to open the document and agree to the certificate (seen in analytical results), PARCEL cannot convert it correctly. To rectify, delete and re-upload the document to PARCEL. If that doesn't work, try reprinting the PDF and then uploading it. You can also try to get another copy from the source

You did not check off that appendix on the Assemble Deliverable screen Make sure that you have checked all of the sections that you want to include in the final PDF.
